Rules

Ruckus in the Chicken Coop.

To win the game, you need to defeat the final boss with at least 3 bottle throws.

Additionally, you should have collected at least 5 coins.

But beware, the final boss sends out his chickens.

You can eliminate them by jumping on them.

Good luck!
